Getting started

New to Vedetor? Here's the whole setup

No technical knowledge needed — most people finish in under five minutes.

01

Download the app

Install Vedetor on both phones — yours and the one you'll monitor — from Google Play, Cafe Bazaar, or the direct download link.

02

Choose a role

On your own phone pick "Guardian." On the other phone, pick "Monitored device" and accept the on-screen consent notice.

03

Pair the devices

Enter the monitored device's ID (shown on its screen) into your Guardian app. The connection is instant.

04

You're set

Location, status and controls now appear on your dashboard. Upgrade any time for live tracking and remote commands.
