Frequently asked questions

How do I install it?

From Cafe Bazaar, Google Play, or the direct download link on this site — all three carry the same version of the app.

Is my data safe?

Data only ever flows between the monitored device and the guardian — it's never sold or shared with a third party.

What's the domestic server?

For users who installed via Cafe Bazaar, there's an option to route data through infrastructure inside Iran, so no VPN is ever needed.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes — the Personal plan can be canceled whenever you like, and stays active until the end of the period you already paid for.
